About Me

In this section I will talk about me and my intrest in games and why Im the way i am.

Where is did games start for me?

Games started for me with my Nintendo DS that I once got for my birthday. Lets just say I fell in love with games such as the PoKeMon series such as platium and white and black being my most played. I have countless memories playing this games late in the night way past bed time. This game console was my life for year upgrading to a DSI when it came out just so I could have a camera and because my other was beginning to look old. After all this I was one of those kids that didnt get a console i got my first laptop and took up the games Minecraft. Truely one of the best games in excistence, this game kept me entertain for years.

What Happened to me after

Well after playing years of minecraft I went to a gaming event and got to see live CSGO pro level matches and got to play myself. I instantly fell in love with the shooter and have been addicted to it for years. (unfortunately i still play it today). In 2016 one game that came out changed my life. DOOM. This game caught my eye when I saw the brutal glory kills and the fast gameplay which I thought would suit my fast and agile playstyle. Lets just say I loved it.

Other things in my life apart from games

Apart from games I have gained a keen intrest in PC hardware. This is made me wanna build multiple computers from the age of twelve and still saving to buy more PC componets. Also in my life I took up Karate. I started at the age of Ten and worked all the way to black belt. This allowed me to compete at national level adn win gold twice at fighting competions.

Epic Life Changing Things

Apart from doing all that I was an exploer. This is one of my best life experience. I met so many amazing people and learnt so many amazing skills. In the last couple of years I had to take a leadership role due to are leader basically giving up. This meant I had to run nights and help with planning a prep for bigger projects. One of my greatest achievements was helping fundraising from a group trip to spain where we got to do a bunch of activities for two weeks. Being in such a important role really grew me as a person and litterally changed my life.
