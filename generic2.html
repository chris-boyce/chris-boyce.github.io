DirectX11 Project

This is my first took into DirectX11 and the framework. I will be following my tutorial and then expanding upon them once completed.

The Base Project

The base project starts at making a cube by hardcoding a cube vertices and indies and trying to get them to output. After that, I then applied transformations to them to learn how to use them to make a mini solar system. Afterward, I learned how to add different shapes such as pyramids and planes. I also added different views such as wireframe. It was also the first time I calculated lighting using diffuse, ambient, and specular lighting. I then used vertex shading and per-pixel shading.
